MADHYA PRADESH TOURISM

Madhya Pradesh is a central Indian state known for its rich history, wildlife, and culture. Famous attractions include Khajuraho Temples, Sanchi Stupa, and Kanha National Park. It offers beautiful landscapes, heritage sites, and diverse traditions, making it a popular tourist destination. Tourism in Madhya Pradesh has been an attraction of India because of its location in the centre of the country. Madhya Pradesh has won Best Tourism State National award for 3 consecutive years i.e. 2017, 2016 and 2015.[1]
